## Key UX Features
1. **Smooth Transitions**: Gold accent on hover for interactive elements
2. **Consistent Cards**: White cards with beige borders throughout
3. **Status Indicators**: Color-coded badges (Green=available, Amber=pending, Red=rented)
4. **Progress Tracking**: Visual step indicators for custom orders
5. **Responsive Grid**: Adapts from 3-column to single-column on mobile
6. **Search & Filter**: Quick access to find gowns and orders
7. **AI Integration**: Smart measurement and color recommendation prompts
8. **Real-time Updates**: Admin dashboard shows live inventory and rental status

## Typography
- **Headings**: Light weight (300-400) for elegance
- **Body**: Regular weight (400) for readability
- **Accents**: Medium weight (500) for emphasis
- **Scale**: Responsive sizing (text-sm to text-4xl)
